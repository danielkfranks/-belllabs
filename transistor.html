The Transistor: Small Device, Huge Impact

You know, the transistor... it's a tiny semiconductor device that can amplify or switch electronic signals. But don't let its size fool you! It's one of the most important inventions of the 20th century, and it all started at Bell Labs in 1947!

The transistor totally changed electronics, replacing those big, power-hungry vacuum tubes with something smaller, more reliable, and way more efficient. It was a real revolution!
