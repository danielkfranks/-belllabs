Plan 9 Operating System

The Future of Distributed Computing

Introduction

Plan 9 from Bell Labs is a distributed operating system, designed as the successor to Unix by the same group of people who created Unix. It aims to provide a more coherent and integrated environment for distributed computing.

Features

  • Unified Resource Access
  • Advanced File System
  • Distributed Computing Capabilities
  • Improved Security and Performance
