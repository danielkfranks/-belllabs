AT&T Network Operations Center

The Heart of AT&T's Network Management

Overview

The AT&T Network Operations Center (NOC) is the central hub for monitoring and managing AT&T's vast telecommunications network. It plays a crucial role in ensuring the reliability and performance of the network, handling everything from routine maintenance to emergency response.
